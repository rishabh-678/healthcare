About us

Welcome To Health Care Services

Health Care Services (HCS) marks a new beginning for pathology services, offering a wide-ranging of advanced tests

HCS is committed to provide healthcare services with precision following EQA and QC/QA Schemes stringently. To ensure compliance with the quality standards, HCS has created a scientific advisory board (SAB).

SAB is a team of the experienced personage who evaluate the clinical staff on their compliances and further educate them about recent advancements in medical care and laboratory science.

Our goal is to encourage people to take simple yet essential steps towards a longer, healthier and happier living.
